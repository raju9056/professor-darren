Graphic Novels/Vizling

DeFrain has taught graphic novels at Wichita State for many years.

He is the co-author of A Walk with Wu: The Story of Wichita State University (with Jessica Cerri, Jay Price, and Genna Pennington) and the Vizling comic (with Aaron Rodriguez and Genna Pennington).

He is currently at work on a long-form graphic narrative.

DeFrain launched Vizling in 2020 with Aaron Rodriguez. This app makes graphic narratives of all kinds fully accessible and searchable.
